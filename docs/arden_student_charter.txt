Document: Arden University Student Charter
Institution: Arden University
Document Type: Student Charter
Version: Current (as published on official website)

SECTION: Purpose
The Student Charter outlines the values, expectations, and shared responsibilities
that support student success, wellbeing, and safety within Arden University’s
learning community.

SECTION: Principles of the Student Charter
The Student Charter is based on the following principles:
- Prioritising excellent learning, teaching, and support
- Championing respect and inclusion
- Providing meaningful academic and pastoral support
- Fostering engagement and a sense of community
- Empowering and valuing the student voice

SECTION: What Arden University Will Do
Arden University commits to:
- Delivering high-quality and engaging teaching and learning materials
- Communicating clearly, transparently, and in a timely manner
- Providing appropriate academic and pastoral support services
- Creating opportunities for student interaction and community building
- Encouraging and responding to student feedback
- Ensuring students are treated fairly, respectfully, and with dignity

SECTION: What Students Are Expected To Do
Students are expected to:
- Treat staff, fellow students, and the wider community with respect
- Follow relevant university policies, regulations, and procedures
- Engage actively with their academic programme
- Participate in scheduled learning activities
- Seek support when needed and make use of available services
- Contribute positively to the Arden University community

SECTION: Related Policies and Standards
The Student Charter operates alongside the following policies and procedures:
- Student Attendance and Engagement Policy
- Academic Integrity Policy
- Bullying, Harassment and Sexual Misconduct Policy
- Equality, Diversity and Inclusion Policy
- Freedom of Speech Code of Practice
- Safeguarding Policy
- Student Complaints Procedure
- Student Disciplinary Procedure

SECTION: Student Support Services
Arden University provides access to a range of support services, including:
- Academic Skills support
- Careers and Employability services
- Student Wellbeing and Mental Health support
- Disability and accessibility support

END OF DOCUMENT
